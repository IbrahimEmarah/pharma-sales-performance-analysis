# Pharmaceutical Sales & Financial Strategy Analysis (Interactive Dashboard creation using MS Excel)

## Project Objective
The pharmaceutical company aimed to evaluate its sales performance across seven months to identify high-performing products and regional growth opportunities. The objective was to provide the Sales Director with a "Command Center" to visualize the EGP 129.6M portfolio and optimize resource allocation for the upcoming fiscal year.

## Dataset used
- Dataset: <a href="https://github.com/IbrahimEmarah/pharma-sales-performance-analysis/blob/main/Pharmaceutical%20Sales%20Analysis%20Datasheets.xlsx">Internal Pharmaceutical Sales & Operational Audit Data.</a>
- Scope: 7 Months of transactional data across multiple production lines and regions.

## Questions (KPIs)
- What is the total revenue generated, and how does it trend over the 7 months?
- Which region (e.g., Heliopolis, Maadi) is leading in sales volume?
- "Champion vs. Future" Analysis: Which products are core revenue drivers (Champions) and which have high growth potential (Future)?
- How are the top 3 production lines performing in terms of "Operational Excellence"?
- What is the performance rating (1-5 stars) of Area Managers and Employees based on internal audit KPIs?
- Which sales channels or medical sectors are contributing most to the EGP 129.6M turnover?
- Dashboard Interaction <a href="https://github.com/IbrahimEmarah/pharma-sales-performance-analysis/blob/main/Pharmaceutical%20Sales%20Dashboard.png">View Dashboard</a>

## Process
- Data Cleaning: Used Power Query to handle large-scale medical datasets, ensuring consistent naming conventions for pharmaceutical SKUs and removing anomalies.
- Financial Modeling: Applied advanced Excel formulas to calculate the "Champion vs. Future" segmentation, identifying that core products generated 66% (EGP 85.8M) of total turnover.
- Pivot Table Architecture: Built complex pivot tables to correlate regional sales with manager performance ratings.
- Interactive Design: Integrated dynamic Slicers for "Zone" and "Manager Name" to allow for deep-dive regional audits.

![Screenshot of Final Interactive Pharmaceutical Sales Dashboard](https://github.com/IbrahimEmarah/pharma-sales-performance-analysis/blob/main/Pharmaceutical%20Sales%20Dashboard.png)

## Project Insight
- Core Revenue Drivers: The "Champion" product segment is the primary engine of the business, accounting for over EGP 85M of the total revenue.
- Regional Leader: Heliopolis emerged as the top-performing region, contributing EGP 7.8M, indicating a high density of successful medical sector partnerships.
- Operational Quality: The top three production lines maintained high operational standards, but audit data revealed a performance gap in mid-tier managers that requires training intervention.
- Monthly Trends: Sales showed a consistent upward trajectory, peaking in the latter months of the 7-month analysis.

## Final Conclusion
To maximize the EGP 129.6M portfolio's potential, the company should double down on "Champion" products in the Heliopolis and Maadi regions. Additionally, implementing a "Manager Mentorship Program" for the production lines with 3-star ratings could close the operational gap and potentially increase the total turnover by an estimated 10-15% in the next cycle.
